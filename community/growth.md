# Community Growth

> Growth metrics, engagement patterns, and statistics.

## Overview

The Neuko community has grown organically from 0 followers to over 13,000 in just over a month, with strong engagement and diamond-handed holders.

## Growth Statistics

### Followers

- **Started**: 0 (early October 2025)
- **Current**: ~13,462 (as of November 13, 2025)
- **Growth**: ~10K followers in first 2 weeks

### Badge Holders

- **Rabbit Holders**: 250 (Phase 1)

### Engagement

- Average 1K-3K likes per post
- 200-700 reposts

- 100% organic (no paid promotion)
