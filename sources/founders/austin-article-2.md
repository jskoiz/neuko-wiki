Why I'm Here
I got into crypto in 2017 while working on music rights at Amazon. I saw firsthand how centralized systems were built to keep power concentrated at the top. When I discovered people trying to decentralize those systems, to build something designed for equity, not extraction, I was hooked.
I spent years helping independent artists leverage crypto to take back control of their careers. Working with early pioneers like Sound.xyz and Catalog. Building tools for artists the traditional system told couldn't own their own music.
Some of those experiments failed. But the ethos of speaking truth to power and helping the disenfranchised only grew stronger.
When appropriately leveraged, crypto can be an overwhelming force for good. A system designed for equity. A community whose resilience only grows with each bump in the road.
There's a sense of belonging here you cannot replicate anywhere else. If you take the time to connect with your fellow CT goers.
